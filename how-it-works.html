How It Works

1. Request a portfolio

We share a curated portfolio of available artworks for your office or selected private interior every month.

2. Select an artwork

You choose a work that suits the space, mood and visual identity you want to create.

3. Make the deposit*

A refundable deposit is made for the selected artwork. The refundable deposit is made only once at the beginning.

4. Monthly rotation

A monthly fee is paid each time you change the artwork each month. The fee depends on the selected artwork, generally between LKR 3,500 and LKR 10,000.

* Refundable deposit options currently apply at three artwork access levels: LKR 60,000, LKR 100,000 and LKR 150,000.

This service is currently available in Colombo. As the platform grows, artworks and artist participation will expand further across Sri Lanka.
