About Cithambara Diya

Cithambara Diya was created to improve the exposure of Sri Lankan artists and to help build a stronger culture of appreciating art in Sri Lanka.

We believe that original art should not remain hidden in storage, overlooked in private collections, or disconnected from daily life. It should be seen, lived with and given the space to influence the environments in which people work, think and gather. Through artwork placement and rotation, Cithambara Diya brings Sri Lankan art into offices and other carefully selected spaces where it can be continuously experienced.

At the same time, we want to contribute to a broader cultural shift. We believe the quality of life in a country is shaped not only by economics and convenience, but also by taste, atmosphere, imagination and the ability to value meaningful forms of beauty. By encouraging companies and individuals to engage with original Sri Lankan art, we hope to strengthen a more thoughtful and elevated cultural environment.

We are beginning in Colombo and slowly expanding to different parts of Sri Lanka. Our present focus is on visual art, while future phases will extend into literature and other cultural forms. Cithambara Diya is therefore not only about displaying art. It is about expanding appreciation, creating opportunity and helping culture take root more deeply in everyday Sri Lankan life.
