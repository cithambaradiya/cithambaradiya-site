Pastel Valleys

Artist: Iranganie Wickramasinghe

Medium: Acrylic on canvas

Dimensions: 102.87 cm (H) × 96.52 cm (W)

Pastel Valleys is a tranquil abstract landscape bathed in soft, delicate hues of lavender, blush and sky blue. Gentle rolling hills, distant peaks and open skies blend seamlessly through layers of pastel tones, creating an atmosphere of calm and serenity.
