Harvest of Grace

Serene Alexie Adams 70 cm (H) 120 cm (W)

Artist: Alexie Adams

Dimensions: 70 cm (H) 120 cm (W)

Category: Serene

Description: Beneath the golden morning light, graceful women wade through the tranquil river, plucking lotus flowers with skilled hands. Their baskets brim with blossoms, each petal a symbol of labor, tradition, and nature’s quiet beauty.
