Feminine Rage

Artist: Fatema Kaizar

Medium: Acrylic on canvas

Dimensions: 35 cm (H) × 25 cm (W)

“Almost every woman I have ever met has a secret belief that she is just on the edge of madness. That there is some deep, crazy part within her that she must be on guard constantly against: losing control of her temper, of her appetite, of her feelings, of her ambition, of her secret fantasies, of her mind.”

— Elana Dykewomon
